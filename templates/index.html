What is Drunir ?

A Minecraft mod about a Magical Dimension !

Drunirs is a Minecraft Mod about a Magical Dimension full of Mythological Creatures. With the addition of mythical beasts like dragons and unicorns , players can explore a whole new world of possibilities !

With new abilities, items, and challenges, Drunirs promises to bring a fresh and exciting dimension to Minecraft gameplay.
